#NoFilter

A Google Chrome extension aimed at filtering all your feeds from any unwanted content
Created by Vishnu Chopra

What is #NoFilter?

#NoFilter is a small project I took up due to some tragic events in my personal life.

In the summer of 2017 I discovered the incredible world of Game of Thrones, and was enjoying the mind-boggling journey until ...

You guessed it. Spoilers.

It seems people these days on social media just have no filter (hence the name). So to save people like me from spoilers about TV shows, movies or sporting events, or just to even block out any content from their feed about any topic they don't want to hear about, I began building this extension.

This app uses a few layers of natural language processing, along with some help from APIs, to try to rid your feed of any keywords which you don't want to hear about, as well as any terms or phrases related to those keywords.
